# Drizzt Do'Urden

## Summary

Drizzt Do'Urden is a legendary drow ranger of the North, famous for forsaking the evil ways of his people in Menzoberranzan to walk the surface world.

### Details

**Type:** NPC
**Name:** Drizzt Do'Urden
**Also known as:** The Drow of Icewind Dale
**Class:** Ranger / Fighter
**Race:** Drow (Dark Elf)
**Creature type:** Humanoid
**Alignment:** Chaotic Good
**Deity:** Mielikki
**Gender:** Male
**Languages:** Elvish, Common, Undercommon

## Biography

### Background

Born in the Underdark city of Menzoberranzan, Drizzt fled the spider-queen Lolth's oppressive society. He eventually settled in Icewind Dale and became a hero of the North alongside the Companions of the Hall.

### Current role

In the current campaign, Drizzt is acting as a temporary guide for the party as they navigate the Spine of the World.

### Notable events

- Guided the party through the blizzard near Ten-Towns (Session 4).
- Defended the camp against a Yeti ambush, slaying the matriarch (Session 5).

## Physical Description

### Appearance

Drizzt has obsidian skin, stark white hair, and striking lavender eyes. He wears a dark green cloak bordered with fur and wields two scimitars, Icingdeath and Twinkle.

### Distinguishing traits

He is quiet, deeply philosophical, and possesses an intense, focused combat stance.

## Character Information

### Abilities

Master dual-wielder, innate drow magic (globe of darkness, faerie fire), and deep survivalist knowledge.

- **Twinkle:** Defending scimitar.
