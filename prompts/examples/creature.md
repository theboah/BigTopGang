# Mind Flayer (Illithid)

## Summary

Mind flayers, also known as illithids, are incredibly intelligent, psionic aberrations feared throughout the Underdark for their diet of humanoid brains.

### Details

**Type:** Aberration
**Size:** Medium
**Habitat:** The Underdark, Astral Plane
**Alignment:** Lawful Evil

## Description

### Appearance

Humanoid in build, but with a horrifying, octopus-like head sporting four tentacles that surround a lamprey-like mouth. Their skin is smooth, moist, and typically a pale mauve or purplish color.

### Behavior

Cold, calculating, and utterly alien. They view all other races as thralls or livestock. They communicate entirely via telepathy.

## History

### Origin

Illithids are believed to be time-traveling refugees from a far-future empire that spans the multiverse, though their exact origins are a mystery.

### Notable events

- A lone mind flayer was discovered experimenting on duergar in the abandoned mithril mines (Session 8).
- The party barely escaped its psychic blasts and fled back to the surface (Session 8).

## Abilities

### Powers

- **Mind Blast:** A conical wave of psychic energy that stuns enemies.
- **Extract Brain:** Instant lethal damage to incapacitated foes.
- **Telepathy & Domination:** Can enslave weaker minds to act as thralls.

### Weaknesses

Arrogance, reliance on thralls for physical labor, and lack of strong physical melee defense.
