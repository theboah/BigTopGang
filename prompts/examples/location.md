# Waterdeep

## Summary

Waterdeep, also known as the City of Splendors, is the most important and influential city in the North and perhaps in all of Faerûn.

### Details

**Type:** Metropolis
**Size:** Massive (Over 130,000 residents)
**Region:** The Sword Coast
**Economy:** Trade, crafting, adventuring, maritime commerce
**Inhabitants:** Humans, Elves, Dwarves, Halflings, and countless others.

## History

### Lore

Waterdeep was built on the deepwater harbor that gives the city its name. It is a hub of commerce, known for its strict code of laws enforced by the City Watch, and its massive subterranean dungeon, Undermountain, created by the Mad Mage Halaster Blackcloak.

### Recent events

- The party arrived at the South Gate and secured lodging at the Yawning Portal (Session 12).
- A localized earthquake, rumored to be caused by a disturbance in Undermountain, damaged the Dock Ward (Session 13).

## Geographic Features

### Feature

- **Mount Waterdeep:** A towering peak providing a natural barrier against the sea, housing Griffon Cavalry roosts.
- **Deepwater Harbor:** A naturally sheltered harbor that fuels the city's massive trade empire.

## Leadership

The city is ruled by the **Masked Lords of Waterdeep**, a council whose identities are kept secret to prevent corruption, led by the **Open Lord**, Laeral Silverhand.

## Military

### Division

- **The City Watch:** Acts as a police force maintaining everyday order.
- **The City Guard:** Acts as the military, defending the walls and gates.
- **The Griffon Cavalry:** Elite airborne knights.

## Notable People

### Person

- **Laeral Silverhand:** Open Lord of Waterdeep.
- **Vajra Safahr:** The Blackstaff, archmage of the city.
- **Durnan:** Proprietor of the Yawning Portal inn.
