### Background

[[Characters/Gaslake|gaslake]] resides in a cottage in the [[Locations/Mithundur Elvish Kingdom|mithundur elvish kingdom]], located in the [[Locations/Kumunthul Forest|kumunthul forest]]. The area around her cottage is noted for its enormous pumpkins, which appear to be enchanted by magic, suggesting a significant and beautiful garden.

### Current role

She plays an initial role in the campaign, residing in her cottage. She was involved in predicting the adventurers' arrival, noting that she had seen them in a vision.

### Notable events

*   **Summoning the Adventurers:** [[Characters/Gaslake|gaslake]] was present at the cottage where three mysterious adventurers ([[Characters/Krieg|krieg]], [[Characters/Izzy|izzy]], and [[Characters/Gildane|gildane]]) were summoned. She used her vision to send them on a quest to the [[Events/Witchlight Carnival|witchlight carnival]].
