# Guslug

## Summary
[[Characters/Guslug|guslug]] is an elf and warlock, notably serving as a warlock of [[Characters/Lorwyn|lorwyn]]. He is described as being mysterious and possessing a strikingly beautiful appearance, despite his true age. He was involved in guiding a group of adventurers toward the [[Events/Witchlight Carnival|witchlight carnival]].

## Biography

### Background
[[Characters/Guslug|guslug]] is associated with [[Characters/Lorwyn|lorwyn]], whom he serves as a warlock. He possesses an air of mystery, suggesting a deep connection to magical or planar forces.

### Current role

He was responsible for giving three mysterious adventurers—[[Characters/Krieg|krieg]], [[Characters/Izzy|izzy]], and [[Characters/Gildane|gildane]]—scrolls of invitation, directing them to the [[Events/Witchlight Carnival|witchlight carnival]]. His actions seem to be connected to a quest to investigate [[Characters/Lorwyn|lorwyn]], his patron.

### Notable events

*   **The Quest to [[Events/Witchlight Carnival|witchlight carnival]]:** [[Characters/Guslug|guslug]] informed three adventurers of a vision, sending them on a quest to reach the [[Events/Witchlight Carnival|witchlight carnival]] and gain access to a [[Items/Feywild portal|feywild portal]] to investigate [[Characters/Lorwyn|lorwyn]].
