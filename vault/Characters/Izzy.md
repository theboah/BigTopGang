[[Characters/Izzy|izzy]] is a [[Characters/drow sorcerer|drow sorcerer]] who was part of a group of adventurers summoned to a cottage in the [[Locations/Mithundur Elvish Kingdom|mithundur elvish kingdom]]. ## Biography

### Background

[[Characters/Izzy|izzy]] was among a group of adventurers who were mysteriously summoned to a cottage in the [[Locations/Mithundur Elvish Kingdom|mithundur elvish kingdom]], located in the [[Locations/Kumunthul Forest|kumunthul forest]]. This gathering was connected to a quest to investigate [[Characters/Lorwyn|lorwyn]].

### Current role

She is an adventurer who participates in the ongoing quest to gain access to a [[Items/Feywild portal|feywild portal]] by visiting the [[Events/Witchlight Carnival|witchlight carnival]].

### Notable events

[[Characters/Izzy|izzy]] is observed early in the storyline at Gaslag's cottage, joining a trio of adventurers (including [[Characters/Krieg|krieg]] and [[Characters/Gildane|gildane]]) who are embarking on a quest set by Gaslag.
