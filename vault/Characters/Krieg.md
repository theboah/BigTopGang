### Current role

[[Characters/Krieg|krieg]] was one of three mysterious adventurers summoned to [[Characters/Gaslake|gaslake]]'s cottage in the [[Locations/Mithundur Elvish Kingdom|mithundur elvish kingdom]]. He subsequently attended the [[Events/Witchlight Carnival|witchlight carnival]], where he participated in an endeavor to keep the carnival's magical aura positive.

### Notable events

*   **The Call to [[Characters/Gaslake|gaslake]]'s Cottage:** Along with two other mysterious adventurers ([[Characters/Izzy|izzy]] and [[Characters/Gildane|gildane]]), [[Characters/Krieg|krieg]] arrived at [[Characters/Gaslake|gaslake]]'s cottage in the [[Locations/Mithundur Elvish Kingdom|mithundur elvish kingdom]] after receiving a summons.
*   **The [[Events/Witchlight Carnival|witchlight carnival]]:** [[Characters/Krieg|krieg]] traveled with his companions to the [[Events/Witchlight Carnival|witchlight carnival]], a traveling carnival that crosses different planes of the multiverse, to help the group achieve a goal related to [[Characters/Mr|mr]].
