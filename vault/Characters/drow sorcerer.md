## Biography

### Background

[[Characters/Izzy|izzy]] was one of the three mysterious adventurers who arrived at Gaslag's cottage in the [[Locations/Mithundur Elvish Kingdom|mithundur elvish kingdom]]. She joined a group of adventurers focused on investigating a potential job and later participating in a quest to gain access to a [[Items/Feywild portal|feywild portal]] via the [[Events/Witchlight Carnival|witchlight carnival]].

### Current role

She was an active participant in the initial gathering of adventurers and the subsequent quest to the [[Events/Witchlight Carnival|witchlight carnival]], where she was part of a team alongside [[Characters/Krieg|krieg]] and [[Characters/Gildane|gildane]].

### Notable events

*   **Gaslag's Cottage:** She arrived with two other mysterious adventurers ([[Characters/Krieg|krieg]] and [[Characters/Gildane|gildane]]) at a cottage in the [[Locations/Mithundur Elvish Kingdom|mithundur elvish kingdom]]. 
*   **[[Events/Witchlight Carnival|witchlight carnival]]:** She attended the [[Events/Witchlight Carnival|witchlight carnival]], where the group was tasked with making the carnival as happy as possible to gain an audience with [[Characters/Mr|mr]]. Witch and [[Characters/Mr|mr]]. Light.
