# Witchlight Carnival

## Summary

The [[Events/Witchlight Carnival|witchlight carnival]] is a traveling carnival that traverses between different planes of the multiverse. It arrived on the [[Locations/material plane|material plane]] as part of a quest for several adventurers, orchestrated by [[Characters/Guslug|guslug]], who sent them to gain access to a [[Items/Feywild portal|feywild portal]]. The carnival has a unique, powerful magical aura that amplifies the emotional state of its attendees; collective happiness enhances the entire carnival, while sadness causes a pervasive sorrow.

## Overview

The carnival provides a journey that requires the participants to manage and amplify positive emotions to achieve their goals. The goal of the protagonists was to gain an audience with [[Characters/Mr|mr]]. Witch and [[Characters/Mr|mr]]. Light, the two Dark Elf owners of the carnival, to investigate [[Characters/Lorwyn|lorwyn]], the patron of [[Characters/Guslug|guslug]].

### What happened

Three adventurers were summoned by [[Characters/Guslug|guslug]] and sent on a quest that led them to the [[Events/Witchlight Carnival|witchlight carnival]]. The carnival, which travels between planes, temporarily arrived on the [[Locations/material plane|material plane]]. The attendees are given enchanted witch-like wings and are immersed in a magical environment where emotional energy dictates the mood of the entire event. The group realized that their mission required them to actively generate happiness to succeed.

### Outcome

The characters successfully navigate the emotional currents of the carnival, using their combined efforts to keep the atmosphere cheerful and productive, allowing them to advance their mission objectives.

## Chronology

### Beginning

The adventurers were summoned to Gaslag's cottage in the [[Locations/Mithundur Elvish Kingdom|mithundur elvish kingdom]] and were given scrolls of invitation for the carnival as part of a quest to investigate [[Characters/Lorwyn|lorwyn]].

### Middle

After a week of traveling, the group arrived at the carnival. They were guided into the festivities, where they encountered key figures such as the VIP tour guide Stacy and the enigma [[Characters/Rachel|rachel]]. They learned of the carnival's magical nature—that collective feelings dictate the environment.
