# Feywild Portal

## Summary

[[Items/Feywild portal|feywild portal]] is an object or mechanism allowing passage to the Feywild plane. It is utilized in the quest to investigate [[Characters/Lorwyn|lorwyn]] and gain access to the realm.

### Details

#### Type

Other

#### Rarity

unknown

#### Item type

A portal or magical gateway.

#### Owner

Unknown (or used by the characters)

## Description

### Appearance

Details are not specified, but it serves as a gateway to the Feywild.

### Properties

Allows passage from the [[Locations/material plane|material plane]] into the Feywild. Its existence is key to the quest involving [[Characters/Lorwyn|lorwyn]].

## History

### Origin

Mentioned in relation to the objectives set by Gaslag and [[Characters/Guslug|guslug]].

### Notable events

The characters are sent to the [[Events/Witchlight Carnival|witchlight carnival]] specifically to gain access to this portal.

## Use

### Function

To serve as an entry point for characters to investigate the Feywild realm and [[Characters/Lorwyn|lorwyn]].

### Limitations

The means of access is linked to the overall objectives of the group's quest.
