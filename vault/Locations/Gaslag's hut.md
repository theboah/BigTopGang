# Gaslag's hut

## Summary

[[Locations/Gaslag's hut|gaslag's hut]] is a cottage located in the [[Locations/Kumunthul Forest|kumunthul forest]], within the [[Locations/Mithundur Elvish Kingdom|mithundur elvish kingdom]]. It served as the starting point for an encounter involving three adventurers, who were drawn there for a potential quest.

## History

### Lore

[[Locations/Gaslag's hut|gaslag's hut]] was the initial setting for the quest that involved the three adventurers. The location was noted for its unusual garden and its association with mystical events.

### Recent events

Three mysterious adventurers ([[Characters/Krieg|krieg]], [[Characters/Izzy|izzy]], and [[Characters/Gildane|gildane]]) arrived at the hut, presumably summoned by the need for a quest. Gaslag later sends them on a quest to the [[Events/Witchlight Carnival|witchlight carnival]] to gain access to a [[Items/Feywild portal|feywild portal]].
