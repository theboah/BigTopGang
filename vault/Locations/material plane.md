# Material Plane

## Summary

The [[Locations/material plane|material plane]] is one of the planes of existence within the multiverse. It is the plane generally considered to be the primary reality or the default setting for most mortal stories, often serving as a point of interaction between different realms.

### Details

#### Type

Primary reality/Planar location

#### Size

Varies, serving as the primary setting for mortal civilizations.

#### Population

Highly diverse, ranging from mortal settlements to various magical inhabitants.

#### Location

It constitutes a primary dimensional location in the multiverse.

#### Economy

Standard terrestrial economies, supporting diverse societies and trade.

#### Inhabitants

Mortals, various planar beings, and magical entities who exist within the primary dimensional reality.

## History

The [[Locations/material plane|material plane]] serves as a stable backdrop for much of the recorded narrative, interacting with other planes like the Feywild, as seen with the arrival of the [[Events/Witchlight Carnival|witchlight carnival]].

### Lore

It is the core dimension where most recorded historical events take place, making it a common point of convergence for magical and planar travelers.

### Founding

Unknown, generally considered a fundamental reality of the multiverse.

### Recent events

The [[Locations/material plane|material plane]] served as the arrival point for the [[Events/Witchlight Carnival|witchlight carnival]], a traveling magical event between planes.

## Geographic Features

### Feature

Diverse geographical features ranging from forests (such as the [[Locations/Mithundur Elvish Kingdom|mithundur elvish kingdom]] in the [[Locations/Kumunthul Forest|kumunthul forest]]) to urban centers, supporting varied cultures and ecosystems.
