# Session 1 Summary

The session summary details the beginning of the Big Top Gang's journey. The story begins at Gaslake's cottage in the Mythendul Elvish Kingdom, where three adventurers (Krieg, Izzy, and Gildane) are gathered. Gaslag sends them on a quest to the Witchlight Carnival to gain access to a Feywild portal and investigate Lorwyn, Gaslug's patron. After traveling for a week, they arrive at the traveling carnival, where they are guided by the wood elf ranger Stacy and meet the enigmatic Rachel. They receive special 'Witchlight Wings' and learn that the carnival's magical aura amplifies emotional feelings. The group plans to keep the carnival happy to gain an audience with the dark elf owners, Mr. and Mr. Witchlight.
