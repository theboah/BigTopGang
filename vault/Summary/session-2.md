# Session 2 Summary

The summary details the Big Top Gang's journey, starting at Gaslag's cottage in the Mithundur Elvish Kingdom. Three mysterious adventurers—Krieg (artificer), Izzy (drow sorcerer), and Gildane (half-elf paladin)—receive a quest from Gaslag and are sent to the Witchlight Carnival to gain access to a Feywild portal to investigate Lorwyn, Gaslag's patron warlock. Upon arrival, they meet a wood elf ranger named Stacey and a vibrant character named Rachel, who also joins the VIP tour. They enter the magical carnival, which is attuned to the emotions of its attendees, and plot to generate happiness to gain an audience with the owners, Mr. Witch and Mr. Light.
