# Session 4 Summary

The session summary begins at Gaslag's cottage in the Mithundur Elvish Kingdom, where three adventurers (Krieg, Izzy, and Gildane) are summoned. Gaslag, who is connected to Lorwyn (a warlock) and acts as the patron for the quest, sends the adventurers to the Witchlight Carnival to investigate Lorwyn. The adventurers arrive at the carnival and are met by the wood elf ranger Stacy, who guides them through the VIP tour. They enter the magical carnival and learn that its atmosphere reflects the emotions of its attendees. The group plans to keep the carnival happy to gain an audience with the Dark Elf owners, Mr. Witch and Mr. Light.
