# Session 5 Summary

The campaign started at Gaslag's cottage in the Mithundur Elvish Kingdom, where three adventurers (Krieg, Izzy, and Gildane) were summoned. Gaslag, a warlock of Lorwyn, tasked them with traveling to the Witchlight Carnival to access a Feywild portal and investigate Lorwyn. After a week of travel, the group arrived at the carnival. They were guided by Stacy, a wood elf ranger, and met Rachel, who was also on the VIP tour. They received Witchlight Wings and learned that the carnival's magical aura amplifies the collective emotions of all attendees. The group devised a plan to make the carnival happy to gain an audience with the owners, Mr. Witch and Mr. Light.

## Key Facts

- The initial meeting place was Gaslag's cottage in the Mithundur Elvish Kingdom.
- Gaslag tasked the three adventurers with finding a Feywild portal at the Witchlight Carnival to investigate Lorwyn.
- The adventurers were initially introduced as Krieg (artificer), Izzy (drow sorcerer), and Gildane (half-elf paladin).
- The Witchlight Carnival is a traveling carnival between planes of existence.
- The carnival's atmosphere amplifies the emotions of all attendees.
- The group planned to maintain a happy atmosphere to gain access to Mr. Witch and Mr. Light.
